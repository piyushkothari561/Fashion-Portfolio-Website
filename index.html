This is me

Accessory designing

I work in the fashion industry designing jewellery, bags, belts, scarves and other fashion accessories. Additionally, I study trends, sketch designs and oversee the production of my work.

Modelling

I model garments and other apparel to display clothing before prospective buyers at fashion shows, private showings, retail establishments, or photographer.

Digital art

I use technology to make my artwork come alive. In addition to traditional art techniques, such as drawing and sculpture,I use computer software to create 3-D animation, interactive website graphics and digitally enhanced photographs

Concept designing

I visualize and create artwork in many different mediums, designing art in every phase of its production. I typically work daytime business hours in an art studio or office environment, and primarily report to the production director.
